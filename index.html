Stage 2

Establish safe test conditions

Refrigerant readings are not meaningful until basic operation, airflow, and measurement conditions are verified.

Stop and follow site procedures for electrical hazards, abnormal noise, burning odor, damaged wiring, or an A2L refrigerant alarm. Do not continue live testing when conditions are unsafe.
