Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 2025

HVAC Troubleshooter Pro is a diagnostic and reporting tool used by HVAC technicians. This app does not collect, share, or sell personal information from users. Diagnostic data entered by technicians (system readings, notes, etc.) remain local on the device and may be emailed only when the user chooses to send a report.

Information We Collect

  • Only data you manually enter for HVAC diagnostics
  • No background tracking, location, or analytics

How Data Is Used

Entered data are used solely to generate system reports and are never uploaded automatically.
